1. Our privacy-first approach

Solo Gainz is designed to run entirely on your device. We do not operate user accounts or a game server, and we do not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share your personal data with any server controlled by Xoventic or with any third party for their own purposes. Your hunter profile, workout progress, stats, ranks, inventory, and preferences are created and kept locally on your device and remain under your control.

2. Information stored on your device

The following information is created and stored locally by the App and never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or share it yourself:

  • Your chosen hunter name and optional profile image.
  • Progress data: XP, level, rank, coins, quests, chests, inventory, achievements, and lifetime workout statistics.
  • Activity data used for quests, such as step counts and the distance/pace of a run in progress, processed on-device.
  • App preferences: notification settings, reminder time, language, sound, and interface options.

Because this data is stored locally, deleting the App (or clearing its data) permanently removes it from your device. We do not keep a backup copy, and we cannot recover it for you.

3. What we do not collect

To be clear, Solo Gainz does not: create online accounts; require you to sign in; collect your name, email, phone number, or contacts; build advertising profiles; sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA; or upload your location history, photos, or workout data to us. We have no access to the data stored on your device.

5. Legal bases for processing (GDPR)

Where the GDPR or UK GDPR applies and any processing occurs, it takes place on your device and relies on the following legal bases: your consent (for example, when you grant a permission such as location or camera, which you may withdraw at any time); the performance of our agreement with you to provide the App's features; and our legitimate interests in operating, securing, and improving the App, balanced against your rights. 6. Analytics & advertising

The App does not include third-party advertising, and we do not use cross-app or cross-site tracking, advertising identifiers, or behavioural profiling. We do not integrate third-party analytics SDKs that identify you.

This companion website is served by a static hosting provider that may automatically record standard, aggregated technical logs (such as anonymised request counts, timestamps, approximate region, and error information) for reliability, security, and abuse prevention. These logs are handled by the hosting provider under its own terms, are not combined by us to identify you, and are not used for advertising. The website does not set advertising cookies.

8. Data retention

App data is retained on your device for as long as the App is installed and until you delete it (by clearing the App's data or uninstalling). We do not retain your App data ourselves because we never receive it. Any aggregated hosting logs are retained by the hosting provider for a limited period in line with its standard practices.

9. Data security

Because your data resides on your device, its security is closely tied to your device's own protections (screen lock, operating-system updates, and the permissions you grant). We take reasonable measures in the App's design to protect locally stored data, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for safeguarding access to your device.

10. Children's privacy

Solo Gainz is not directed to children under 13 (or the higher minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided personal information in a way that concerns you, please contact us and we will address it. Because the App stores data locally and has no accounts, parents can remove all such data by clearing the App's data or uninstalling it.

11. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, port, or restrict the processing of your personal data, to object to processing, and to withdraw consent. Because Solo Gainz keeps your data on your device and we do not hold it, you can exercise these rights directly and immediately:

  • Correction: you can edit your hunter name, profile image, and preferences within the App.
  • Deletion: you can delete all App data at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling it.
  • Permissions & consent: you can grant or revoke permissions at any time in your device's system settings.

If you are in the European Economic Area or the UK, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority. If you are a California resident, we do not sell or share your personal information, and we will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.
