What is Solo Gainz?

Solo Gainz is a fitness RPG that turns your training into a hunt. Every rep you log powers a real quest — finish your daily workouts to earn XP, rise through rank shields from E to SG, and prove you're leveling up in the real world, not just the game.

It's more than a tracker. Master a frame-accurate combat system with dedicated punch and kick upgrade trees, then throw down against friends in local Wi‑Fi PVP — no servers, no lag, just two hunters on the same network. Keep your streak alive to crack loot chests from wooden to diamond.

And it's yours, completely: 100% offline, no account, no ads, no tracking. Just you, the grind, and the fight — in your pocket.
